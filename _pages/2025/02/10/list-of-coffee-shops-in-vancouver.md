## **Large/National Chains**

### **1. Starbucks**
- **About**: International coffeehouse chain with countless locations across Vancouver.  
- **Application Link**: [Starbucks Careers](https://www.starbucks.ca/careers)  
- **How to Apply**: Create an online profile, search for Vancouver jobs, and apply directly.

### **2. Tim Hortons**
- **About**: Iconic Canadian coffee-and-baked-goods chain found all over Greater Vancouver.  
- **Application Link**: [Tim Hortons Careers](https://careers.timhortons.ca/)  
- **How to Apply**: Click “Find a Restaurant Job,” filter by Vancouver, and apply online. Some franchise locations also accept in-person applications.

### **3. Second Cup**
- **About**: One of Canada’s original specialty coffee retailers, with select Vancouver locations.  
- **Application Link**: [Second Cup Careers](https://secondcup.com/pages/careers)  
- **How to Apply**: Use their online portal or inquire in person at individual stores.

## **Established BC-Based Chains**

### **4. Blenz Coffee**
- **About**: A Canadian specialty coffee chain founded in Vancouver, with many local spots.  
- **Application Link**: [Blenz Careers](https://blenz.com/careers/)  
- **How to Apply**: Scroll for open roles or submit a general application.

### **5. Waves Coffee House**
- **About**: A Vancouver-based chain known for comfortable lounges and a community vibe.  
- **Application Link**: [Waves Coffee Careers](https://wavescoffee.com/careers/)  
- **How to Apply**: Check their careers page for current job postings or drop off your résumé in person.

### **6. JJ Bean Coffee Roasters**
- **About**: A local roaster and café chain focusing on direct-trade beans and fresh baking.  
- **Application Link**: [JJ Bean Careers](https://jjbeancoffee.com/careers/)  
- **How to Apply**: Apply online; they regularly post barista, bakery, and roasting roles.

### **7. Caffè Artigiano**
- **About**: A locally grown chain noted for artisan espresso drinks (and quality latte art).  
- **Application Link**: [Caffè Artigiano Careers](https://www.caffeartigiano.com/pages/join-our-team)  
- **How to Apply**: Submit your résumé on their “Join Our Team” page or in person at a location.

### **8. Trees Coffee**
- **About**: A smaller Vancouver chain acclaimed for organic coffee and cheesecakes.  
- **Application Link**: [Trees Coffee Careers](https://treescoffee.com/pages/join-the-team)  
- **How to Apply**: Complete the online form or email your résumé to the address provided.

### **9. Bean Around The World**
- **About**: Vancouver-based chain serving house-roasted beans in cozy, laid-back cafés.  
- **Application Link**: [Bean Around The World Careers](https://www.batw.ca/careers)  
- **How to Apply**: Review openings online or submit a general application.

### **10. Prado Cafe**
- **About**: A growing local chain known for specialty coffee and fresh pastries, but still casual.  
- **Website**: [Prado Cafe](https://www.pradocafe.com/)  
- **How to Apply**: Look for “Careers” or “Join Our Team” in the site footer or inquire directly with store managers.

## **Local Chains & Multi-Location Cafés (Not Too Fancy)**

### **11. Delany’s Coffee House**
- **About**: A friendly, homegrown coffee chain with several locations on Vancouver’s West Side and North Shore.  
- **Website**: [Delany's Coffee](https://www.delanyscoffee.com/)  
- **How to Apply**: No dedicated careers link. Typically, drop off a résumé in person or inquire by phone.

### **12. Breka Bakery & Café**
- **About**: A popular 24-hour bakery and café chain with multiple Vancouver locations, known for pastries, sandwiches, and simple coffee options.  
- **Website**: [Breka Bakery & Café](https://breka.ca/)  
- **How to Apply**: Walk-in applications are common. You can also email them using the contact info on their website.

### **13. Take Five Café**
- **About**: A local café chain offering espresso drinks, sandwiches, and baked goods.  
- **Website**: [Take Five Café](https://www.takefivecafe.ca/)  
- **How to Apply**: Check the “Contact” or “Locations” page for details; many stores accept résumés in person.

### **14. Esquires Coffee**
- **About**: Though Esquires has international franchises, it started in BC and still has some Vancouver-area locations.  
- **Website**: [Esquires Coffee (Global)](https://www.esquirescoffee.com/)  
- **How to Apply**: Typically done at the store level. Inquire in person or look for “Now Hiring” signs.

### **15. Caffè Divano**
- **About**: A family-owned café with locations in Burnaby, North Vancouver, and Maple Ridge, featuring coffee, pastries, and light lunches.  
- **Website**: [Caffè Divano](https://caffedivano.com/)  
- **How to Apply**: Often handled at each store; drop off a résumé or use the website’s contact form to inquire.

---

### **General Tips for Applying**
1. **Visit in Person**: Many smaller or locally owned cafés prefer face-to-face introductions. Avoid peak hours when dropping off a résumé.  
2. **Highlight Relevant Skills**: Customer service, food-handling, and teamwork experience are key.  
3. **Flexibility**: Be open to weekends, early mornings, and holiday shifts—cafés often need staff during these times.  
4. **Check Store Windows**: Look for “Help Wanted” or “Now Hiring” signs, especially for local spots.  

Use the links above to explore available positions, and don’t be shy about visiting your favorite coffee shops in person. Good luck with your applications!

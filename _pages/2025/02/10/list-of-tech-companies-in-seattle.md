## 2. Global Tech Giants

### Amazon
**What They Do**  
- E-commerce, cloud computing (AWS), streaming (Prime Video, Twitch), AI (Alexa), consumer devices, and more.

**Roles & Qualities They Look For**  
- **Software Development Engineer (SDE)**: Strong coding (Java/C++/Python), knowledge of distributed systems, AWS experience a plus.  
- **Product Manager (PM)**: Deep customer empathy, strong writing skills (Amazon’s famous narrative documents), ability to drive product vision.  
- **Data Scientist / Data Engineer**: Statistical modeling, ML, big data handling (Redshift, EMR, Spark), and advanced analytics.  
- **UX Designer / Researcher**: User-centered design, portfolio showcasing complex flows, iterative user testing.

**Hiring Process**  
1. **Online Application** at [Amazon Jobs](https://www.amazon.jobs/).  
2. **Online Assessments** (for some roles, especially new graduates or SDE roles).  
3. **Phone/Video Screen** (technical or behavioral).  
4. **On-site / Virtual “Loop”**: Multiple back-to-back interviews, including a “Bar Raiser.” Heavy focus on Amazon’s **Leadership Principles**.  
5. **Offer & Negotiation** with base salary + sign-on bonus + RSUs.

---

### Microsoft
**What They Do**  
- Windows, Office 365, Azure cloud, Surface devices, Xbox, LinkedIn, GitHub, enterprise software.

**Roles & Qualities They Look For**  
- **Software Engineer (SWE)**: Solid CS fundamentals, ability to work in .NET/C#, C++, or other major languages, knowledge of scalable architectures.  
- **Program Manager (PM)**: Communication, cross-functional collaboration, user empathy, and strategic thinking.  
- **Data Scientist / AI Engineer**: Azure ML, Python, big data pipelines, advanced analytics.  
- **Designer / UX Researcher**: UI/UX prototyping, user testing, and an interest in inclusive design.

**Hiring Process**  
1. **Apply** via [Microsoft Careers](https://careers.microsoft.com/).  
2. **Recruiter Screen** to align on role interest and fit.  
3. **Technical / Functional Interview** (phone or Teams).  
4. **Final Interview Loop** (4-5 interviews) assessing coding, system design, PM case studies, or portfolio reviews for design.  
5. **Offer** with base, bonus, stock (SPSA).

---

### Google (Seattle/Kirkland)
**What They Do**  
- Search & advertising, YouTube, Android, Google Cloud, AI research (DeepMind, Brain), hardware (Pixel).

**Roles & Qualities They Look For**  
- **Software Engineer**: Exceptional coding (C++, Java, Python, Go), large-scale systems, strong algorithms, and data structures.  
- **Product Manager**: Analytical thinking, user-focused product development, strong communication, data-driven decisions.  
- **Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)**: Linux expertise, troubleshooting distributed systems, coding for reliability.  
- **ML / AI Engineer**: TensorFlow, ML pipelines, large-scale data.

**Hiring Process**  
1. **Application** through [Google Careers](https://careers.google.com/).  
2. **Technical Phone/Video Screens** (coding, system design).  
3. **On-site / Virtual On-site** with ~4 interviews: data structures/algorithms, system design, “Googleyness” & leadership.  
4. **Hiring Committee** review.  
5. **Offer** (base + bonus + equity) after final approvals.

**What They Do**  
- Consumer electronics (iPhone, iPad, Mac), OS (iOS, macOS), services (iCloud, Apple Music), emerging AI and ML.

**Roles & Qualities They Look For**

4. **Offer** typically includes base, RSUs, and potential signing bonus.

## 8. General Application Tips & Resources

1. **Tailor Your Résumé & Cover Letter**  
   - Highlight **results** (metrics, improvements) for each accomplishment.  
   - Use **keywords** relevant to each role (e.g., “distributed systems,” “microservices,” “DevOps,” “machine learning,” etc.).

2. **Leverage Local Tech Communities**  
   - **Meetups**: New Tech Seattle, Seattle JS, Seattle AI, etc.  
   - **Seattle Angel Conference**: Great for connecting with startups.  
   - **Built In Seattle**: [https://www.builtinseattle.com/](https://www.builtinseattle.com/) for local job postings and tech news.  

3. **Interview Preparation**  
   - **Coding**: Practice on LeetCode, HackerRank, CodeSignal for SDE roles.  
   - **System Design**: Study distributed systems patterns, concurrency, scalability.  
   - **Behavioral**: Use the STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) or similar frameworks.  
   - **Product / PM**: Focus on product sense, user empathy, metrics-driven decisions.

4. **Network, Network, Network**  
   - **LinkedIn**: Connect with recruiters, employees at target companies.  
   - **Conferences & Virtual Events**: Tech conferences (e.g., AWS re:Invent), local job fairs.

5. **Emphasize Culture Fit**  
   - **Amazon**: Leadership Principles.  
   - **Microsoft**: Growth mindset, inclusive environment.  
   - **Startups**: Adaptability, ownership, willingness to wear multiple hats.

6. **Follow-Up**  
   - Send thank-you notes or emails to interviewers (if appropriate).  
   - Reinforce your enthusiasm for the role and reference specific parts of the interview.

Bilibili (哔哩哔哩) offers an English interface for menus but has limited English content.

### **Steps:**
1. Open **Bilibili (哔哩哔哩)**.
2. Tap **"Me" (我)** in the bottom right.
3. Go to **"Settings" (设置)**.
4. Select **"General" (通用设置)**.
5. Tap **"Language" (语言)**.
6. Choose **"English"**.

Some features like subtitles, video descriptions, and comments will still be in Chinese.

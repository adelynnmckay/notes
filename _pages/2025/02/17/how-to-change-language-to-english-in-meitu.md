Meitu (美图) supports English for menus and settings. Follow these steps:

### **Steps:**
1. Open **Meitu (美图)**.
2. Tap the **profile icon** in the bottom right.
3. Select **"Settings" (设置)**.
4. Choose **"Language" (语言设置)**.
5. Tap **"English"** and confirm.

Now the app’s interface will be in English, but some AI effects and community content will remain in Chinese.

Tencent QQ (QQ) offers partial English support, primarily in menus and settings.

### **Steps:**
1. Open **Tencent QQ (QQ)**.
2. Tap **your profile icon** in the top left corner.
3. Scroll down and select **"Settings" (设置)**.
4. Choose **"General" (通用)**.
5. Tap **"Language" (多语言)**.
6. Select **"English"** and confirm.

Note: Many areas, including chat and system notifications, may still appear in Chinese.

WeChat (微信) allows users to switch the in-app language to English. Follow these steps:

### **Steps:**
1. Open **WeChat (微信)**.
2. Tap **"Me" (我)** in the bottom right corner.
3. Select **"Settings" (设置)**.
4. Choose **"General" (通用)**.
5. Tap **"Language" (多语言)**.
6. Scroll and select **"English"**.
7. Tap **"OK" (确定)** to confirm.

WeChat will now display in English. Some third-party mini-programs may still be in Chinese.

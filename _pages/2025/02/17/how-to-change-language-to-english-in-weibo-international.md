Sina Weibo International (微博国际版) has **partial English support**, but some content remains in Chinese. Follow these steps to switch the interface:

### **Steps:**
1. Open **Sina Weibo International (微博国际版)**.
2. Tap your **Profile Icon** at the bottom.
3. Go to **"Settings" (设置)**.
4. Select **"General" (通用设置)**.
5. Tap **"Language" (语言设置)**.
6. Choose **"English"** and confirm.

After switching, menus and settings will appear in English, but posts, comments, and trends will still be mostly in Chinese.

Apps like Venmo, CashApp, Zelle, etc. do not work in Canada. Here is a list of alternatives for sending and receiving money in Canada. Some of them allow only Canadian domestic transfers, and some allow transfers between the United States and Canada. 

Here’s the updated table including **crypto and alternative payment solutions**, while keeping everything structured and indicating whether money can be sent from the US.

---

## **Domestic Money Transfer Apps (Canada)**
These apps are best for **sending money within Canada**:

| **Alternative**         | **Features**                                             | **Transfer Speed**                     | **Fees & Costs**                          | **Can Money Be Sent from the US?** |
|------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| **KOHO**              | Cashback card, budgeting app, credit builder, unlimited transactions | Instant or up to 3 hours | Free and paid plans | ❌ No (Canada-only) |
| **Neo Money**         | Earn cashback, free unlimited transactions       | e-Transfers take up to 45 minutes | Free and paid plans | ❌ No (Canada-only) |
| **Wise**             | Multicurrency accounts, low exchange rate fees | Instant or up to 2 working days | Fees vary by currency/type | ✅ Yes |
| **Wealthsimple Cash** | Pay for purchases, send/receive money, invest | Instant for cash/savings, up to 7 days for other accounts | No monthly fees, but some financial transactions have charges | ❌ No (Canada-only) |
| **PayPal**           | Peer-to-peer money transfers, secure buying/selling transactions | Instant or up to 3 business days | Fees vary by transaction type | ✅ Yes |
| **Interac e-Transfer** | Most common bank-based transfer method | Typically within minutes | Free to receive; sending fees vary by bank | ❌ No (Canada-only) |

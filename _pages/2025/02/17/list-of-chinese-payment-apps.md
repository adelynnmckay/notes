## **Chinese Payment Apps & Cross-Border Availability**
These apps are widely used in **China and among the Chinese diaspora** for peer-to-peer transfers, payments, and international transactions.

| **Payment App**    | **Features**                                  | **Can Send Money to Canada?** | **Can Send Money to the US?** | **Supported Currencies & Regions** | **Transfer Speed** | **Fees & Costs** |
|--------------------|----------------------------------------------|--------------------------|------------------------|--------------------------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **WeChat Pay (微信支付)** | Integrated with WeChat, mobile payments, peer-to-peer transfers, supports international merchants | ✅ Yes (via partner banks & services) | ✅ Yes (via partner banks & services) | CNY, HKD, USD, CAD & others | Instant (WeChat-to-WeChat), 1–5 days for bank transfers | Free for local transfers; international fees vary |
| **Alipay (支付宝)** | Mobile wallet, online purchases, international transfers, supports QR code payments | ✅ Yes (via Alipay+ & partner banks) | ✅ Yes (via Alipay+ & partner banks) | CNY, HKD, USD, CAD & others | Instant for Alipay users, 1–5 days for bank transfers | Free for domestic transfers, fees for international transactions |
| **UnionPay (银联云闪付)** | China’s largest bank card network, cross-border money transfers, supports bank & mobile payments | ✅ Yes (via participating Canadian banks) | ✅ Yes (via participating US banks) | CNY, USD, CAD, and other major currencies | 1–3 business days for bank transfers | Varies by bank & amount |
| **Tencent QQ Wallet (QQ 钱包)** | Wallet linked to QQ, used for digital transactions, supports WeChat Pay integration | ❌ No (not widely supported in North America) | ❌ No (not widely supported in North America) | CNY only (Mainland China) | Instant within QQ Wallet | Free for local transfers, limited international options |
| **LianLian Pay (连连支付)** | Cross-border e-commerce payments, supports transfers from China to foreign accounts | ✅ Yes (for business transactions) | ✅ Yes (for business transactions) | CNY, USD, CAD, and other global currencies | 1–5 business days | Fees depend on transaction type |
| **Airwallex (空中云汇)** | Business-focused global money transfers, supports WeChat & Alipay payments | ✅ Yes (for business and e-commerce) | ✅ Yes (for business and e-commerce) | 50+ currencies including CNY, USD, CAD | 1–3 business days | Competitive exchange rates, low fees |
| **PayEase (易宝支付)** | Cross-border payment processing, supports foreign bank card payments in China | ✅ Yes (for online transactions) | ✅ Yes (for online transactions) | CNY, USD, CAD, EUR, and other global currencies | 1–3 business days | Varies by payment method |

---

### **Key Takeaways:**
- **Best for personal transfers:** **WeChat Pay & Alipay** (widely used, available via partner banks)
- **Best for business payments:** **UnionPay, LianLian Pay, Airwallex**
- **Limited US/Canada support:** **QQ Wallet (mostly China-based)**

Here is a list of job search websites specifically for **software engineering** careers:

### **General Tech Job Boards**  
1. **LinkedIn Jobs** ([linkedin.com/jobs](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs)) – Strong networking features for software engineers.  
2. **Indeed** ([indeed.com](https://www.indeed.com)) – Aggregates job postings, including many software engineering roles.  
3. **Glassdoor** ([glassdoor.com](https://www.glassdoor.com)) – Includes salary insights and company reviews for tech roles.  
4. **ZipRecruiter** ([ziprecruiter.com](https://www.ziprecruiter.com)) – AI-powered job matching for software positions.  
5. **Monster** ([monster.com](https://www.monster.com)) – Offers resume-building tools and software job listings.  
6. **CareerBuilder** ([careerbuilder.com](https://www.careerbuilder.com)) – AI-driven job recommendations for tech roles.  

### **Software Engineering & Developer-Specific Job Boards**  
7. **Stack Overflow Jobs** ([stackoverflow.com/jobs](https://stackoverflow.com/jobs)) – Tech-focused job listings from reputable companies.  
8. **Dice** ([dice.com](https://www.dice.com)) – IT and software engineering job listings, with a focus on mid-to-senior roles.  
9. **GitHub Jobs** ([jobs.github.com](https://jobs.github.com)) – Focused on developer roles, often open-source friendly.  
10. **Turing** ([turing.com](https://www.turing.com)) – AI-matched remote software engineering jobs.  
11. **Arc.dev** ([arc.dev](https://arc.dev)) – Remote jobs for experienced developers.  
12. **HackerRank Jobs** ([hackerrank.com/jobs](https://www.hackerrank.com/jobs)) – Coding challenges lead to job opportunities.  

### **Startup & High-Growth Tech Companies**  
13. **AngelList** ([angel.co/jobs](https://angel.co/jobs)) – Startup-focused software engineering jobs.  
14. **Y Combinator Work at a Startup** ([workatastartup.com](https://www.workatastartup.com)) – Y Combinator-backed startup job listings.  
15. **VentureLoop** ([ventureloop.com](https://www.ventureloop.com)) – Startup and venture-backed company job board.  

### **Remote & Distributed Work for Software Engineers**  
16. **We Work Remotely** ([weworkremotely.com](https://weworkremotely.com)) – Remote-first software engineering jobs.  
17. **Remote OK** ([remoteok.io](https://remoteok.io)) – Aggregates remote software development jobs.  
18. **FlexJobs** ([flexjobs.com](https://www.flexjobs.com)) – Curated remote software job listings.  
19. **Remotive** ([remotive.io](https://remotive.io)) – Startup-focused remote software jobs.  
20. **Working Nomads** ([workingnomads.co/jobs](https://www.workingnomads.co/jobs)) – Tech roles for digital nomads.  
21. **JustRemote** ([justremote.co](https://justremote.co)) – Global software engineering jobs.  

### **Freelance & Contract Software Engineering Jobs**  
22. **Upwork** ([upwork.com](https://www.upwork.com)) – Freelance software development gigs.  
23. **Toptal** ([toptal.com](https://www.toptal.com)) – High-end freelance jobs for top developers.  
24. **Gun.io** ([gun.io](https://www.gun.io)) – Matches experienced developers with freelance contracts.  
25. **PeoplePerHour** ([peopleperhour.com](https://www.peopleperhour.com)) – Freelance and contract software jobs.  
26. **Freelancer** ([freelancer.com](https://www.freelancer.com)) – Various software development gigs.

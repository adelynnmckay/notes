This is a tl;dr master list of US, Canadian, Chinese, and international payment applications, banking services, and cryptocurrency platforms.

Note that I don't and haven't actually use most of these so [YMMV](https://duckduckgo.com/?t=ffab&q=your%20mileage%20may%20vary).

See [List of Canadian Payment Apps](/list-of-canadian-payment-apps) and [List of Canadian Payment Apps](/list-of-canadian-payment-apps) for more details on each of these topics.

## Domestic Platforms & Services

### Canadian Only Apps

- Interac e-Transfer *(Only allows for transfers between Canadian bank accounts, but is the standard for transfers in Canada and is used for basically everything.)*

### United States Only Apps

- CashApp *(Only allows US bank account, can transfer to and from other CashApp accounts.)* 
- Venmo *(Only allows US bank account, can transfer to and from other Venmo Accounts.)* 
- Zelle *(Only allows US bank accounts, can transfer to and from US bank accounts.)* 

## International Platforms & Services

### United States & Canadian Apps

- Wise *(Allows both US and Canadian transfers; also has a cryptocurrency transfer service, allows Canadian bank withdrawals.)*
- PayPal *(Allows international transfers, including between the US and Canada.)*
- XE Money Transfer *(Allows international transfers, including between the US and Canada.)*
- WorldRemit *(Allows international transfers, including between the US and Canada.)*
- MoneyGram *(Allows international transfers, including between the US and Canada.)*
- Remitly *(Allows international transfers, including between the US and Canada.)*
- CurrencyFair *(Allows international transfers, including between the US and Canada.)*
- Currencies Direct *(Allows international transfers, including between the US and Canada.)*
- Western Union *(Allows international transfers, including between the US and Canada.)*
- Instarem *(Allows international transfers, including between the US and Canada.)*

### Chinese Apps

- WeChat Pay *(Chinese payment service, supports Canadian partner banks and participating international merchants.)* 
- Alipay *(Chinese payment service, supports Canadian partner banks and participating international merchants.)* 
- UnionPay *(Chinese payment service, supports Canadian partner banks and participating international merchants.)* 

## Crypto Wallets & Trading Platforms

### Crypto Wallets

#### Bitcoin Only Wallets

- Proton Wallet *(Bitcoin only cryptocurrency wallet, can recieve bitcoin via email.)*

## Crypto Trading Platforms

- Shakepay *(Cryptocurrency transfer service, supports cryptocurrency buying and selling via Canadian bank accounts.)*
- Newton *(Cryptocurrency transfer service, supports cryptocurrency buying and selling via Canadian bank accounts.)*
- Coinbase *(Cryptocurrency transfer service, supports cryptocurrency buying and selling via Canadian bank accounts.)*
- Binance *(Cryptocurrency transfer service, supports cryptocurrency buying and selling via Canadian bank accounts.)*
- Wise *(Allows both US and Canadian transfers; also has a cryptocurrency transfer service, allows Canadian bank withdrawals.)*
